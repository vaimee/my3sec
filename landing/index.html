FAQ

How does it work?

My3Sec is the unique decentralized platform to efficiently manage projects and create skilled talented teams on demand connecting remote workers with the perfect tasks to perform.

Why My3Sec is built on blockchain?

My3Sec uses blockchain to build a network of trustworthy skilled workers by creating a democratic system of reputation based on proven portfolio of projects. By distributing the power along the participant of the network, the value of each worker capabilities is evaluated according to the time spent working which leads to better perspective of career focused on results.

What type of skills are verified on My3Sec?

My3Sec focuses mainly on complex technological projects involving digital high tech skills. It primarily targets high skilled software developers and the coding skills engaged during the development of innovative digital products.

How do I manage a project?

If you are an organization, you can create a new project, describe each tasks in details describing the skills you are looking for and assign them to as many team members as you need. Create your profile as project manager to validate team members, monitor the progress of your project and save time.

How do I create my certified portfolio?

If you are a remote worker simply log into your profile and you will find the pending tasks assigned to you. Register the hours spent on each task daily and get rewards for finishing your job. Your proven skill will be displayed on your page and anyone in the network will be able to see your career progresses. If you are a freelance, you will be also able to create projects and tasks to prove your skills while working.

How do I find talents?

If you are looking for talented professionals you can verify the certified portfolio of any member of the network and get in touch with them through the platform.

How much do I pay?

You pay according to the number of projects and team members you are managing on My3Sec. the more you use it the less you pay! If you are a head-hunter you pay a flat monthly fee according to the usage of the platform.
